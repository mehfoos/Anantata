Open Calculator

Overview

Much of our knowledge of the universe can be described through a set of mathematical equations and numerical constants. This is an attempt at building and maintaing an open source database and calculator of equations and universal constants.

Intent and Design Principles

You can submit additions or edits to the database, and can also programmatically query the database. A minimalist interface is used to keep the website lightweight and compatible with a wide range of applications. You are welcome to use the existing database to create your own interfaces.
